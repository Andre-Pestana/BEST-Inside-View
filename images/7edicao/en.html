BEST Inside View

A chance for the students to contact their future

BEST Inside View is a job shadowing and mentoring event, in which a college student follows an engineer working in his/her area of personal interest for an entire day, closely observing their daily routine. This event's goal is not only to help students understand what professional options are available for them but also the roles they may perform in the future: all this comes to strengthen the link between college students and the job market. Through this initiative, the student gets to understand a little better the way the company works: the working methods, their structure and organization. On the other hand, the visited company gets a bigger exposure to the academic community and a closer contact to the participants.

BEST Inside View

7th Edition




The seventh edition do BEST Inside View officially started on 23/11/2017 with the Opening Cerimony that will took place in the Salão Nobre of Instituto Superior Técnico. Right after it, there was a networking session where students can got in touch with the companies and the opportunities that they bring to the event! When the registration results are announced, you can get in touch with the company that you'll visit and establish your own Inside Day!

The registration period will be extended until 11/02/2018.

You can consult the event's regulation here if you're an IST student and here if you're a FCUL or FFUL student.
